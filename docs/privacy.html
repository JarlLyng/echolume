Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Echolume is a macOS app that turns audio input into real-time visuals. This policy describes how we handle information in relation to the app and this website.

Audio and microphone

Echolume requests access to an audio input source (microphone or audio interface) so it can drive the visuals. All audio is processed on your Mac only. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio.

Crash reporting

Echolume uses Sentry for crash reporting and performance monitoring. When the app crashes or encounters an error, Sentry may collect:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Basic performance metrics (app launch time, screen transitions)

Sentry does not collect audio data, personal information, or any content from your device. Crash data is used solely to diagnose and fix bugs. See Sentry's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling.

Data we do not collect

Echolume does not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal information or account data
  • Audio or any content from your device
  • Usage analytics or advertising identifiers

There is no sign-in, no cloud sync, and no tracking.

This website

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard access logs (e.g. IP, browser) according to their Privacy Statement. We do not use cookies or tracking scripts on this site.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app or site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? See Support for how to get in touch.
